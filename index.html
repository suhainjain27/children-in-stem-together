Children in STEM Together Foundation (CIST Foundation)

Purpose

Our purpose is to ensure that every child, regardless of their background or where they live, has access to quality STEM education.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide children in underserved communities with access to quality STEM education through online tutoring, educational projects, and mentorship.

Organization Description

Children in STEM Together Foundation (CIST Foundation) is a student-founded educational organization with the goal of becoming a nonprofit dedicated to making STEM education more accessible for children in underserved communities.

Long-Term Vision

We envision a world where every child has equal access to quality STEM education and the opportunity to reach their full potential.
