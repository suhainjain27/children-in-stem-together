About Us

Children in STEM Together Foundation (CIST Foundation) is a nonprofit organization dedicated to making STEM education accessible to children everywhere.



Our Vision

A world where every child has the opportunity to explore STEM.

Our Goal

Provide tutoring, projects, and mentorship opportunities.

Our Future
